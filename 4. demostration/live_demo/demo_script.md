# ZaNuri - Live Demo Script

## Demo Overview
**Duration:** 15 minutes  
**Target Audience:** Investors, PLP Assessors, Potential Partners  
**Demo Type:** Live mobile app demonstration

## Pre-Demo Setup (2 minutes)
1. **Welcome & Context Setting**
   - "Good morning/afternoon! Today I'm excited to show you ZaNuri - an intelligent food personalization platform designed specifically for Zambian families."
   - "We're solving the double burden of malnutrition through AI-powered meal planning that's culturally relevant and budget-conscious."

2. **Quick Problem Statement**
   - "In Zambia, 35% of children under 5 are stunted, while obesity and diabetes are rapidly rising"
   - "Current solutions are either too generic or too expensive for most families"

## Demo Flow (12 minutes)

### Part 1: User Onboarding (3 minutes)
**Narrative:** "Let me show you how easy it is to get started with personalized nutrition"

**Actions:**
1. Open ZaNuri app
2. Show splash screen with Zambian-inspired design
3. Begin onboarding process
   - "The app starts with a 2-minute health assessment"
   - Demonstrate answering questions about:
     - Health goals (weight loss, diabetes management)
     - Dietary restrictions (lactose intolerance, vegetarian)
     - Budget constraints (low/medium/high)
     - Family size and preferences

**Key Points to Highlight:**
- "Notice how we ask about local staple foods and cooking habits"
- "The AI is learning the user's unique profile in real-time"

### Part 2: AI Meal Planning (4 minutes)
**Narrative:** "Now watch as our AI creates a personalized weekly meal plan"

**Actions:**
1. Show generated meal plan
   - "Based on the user's profile, our algorithm has created a 7-day plan"
   - Highlight Zambian staples: "You'll see nshima, kapenta, local vegetables"
2. Demonstrate plan customization
   - "Don't like a meal? Tap to regenerate alternatives"
   - "All recipes use locally available ingredients"
3. Show nutritional analysis
   - "The plan meets specific health goals while staying within budget"

**Key Points to Highlight:**
- "Each meal costs under ZMW 25 per serving"
- "Recipes respect cultural preferences and cooking time constraints"

### Part 3: Smart Shopping & Vendor Integration (3 minutes)
**Narrative:** "Now let's see how we make grocery shopping smarter"

**Actions:**
1. Generate shopping list from meal plan
   - "The app automatically creates an optimized shopping list"
   - Show grouping by category and vendor
2. Demonstrate vendor marketplace
   - "Find nearby vendors with the best prices"
   - Show Soweto Market integration
3. Demonstrate budget tracking
   - "Total estimated cost: ZMW 245 for the week"
   - "We help families save 20-30% on food waste"

**Key Points to Highlight:**
- "We connect users directly with local farmers and markets"
- "Smart lists reduce impulse buying and food waste"

### Part 4: Progress Tracking (2 minutes)
**Narrative:** "Finally, let's see how users track their health journey"

**Actions:**
1. Show progress dashboard
   - "Users can log meals, water intake, and weight"
2. Demonstrate photo progress tracking
   - "Visual progress helps maintain motivation"
3. Show achievement system
   - "Gamification keeps users engaged"

**Key Points to Highlight:**
- "85% of beta users reported improved eating habits"
- "The system learns and adapts to user preferences over time"

## Demo Conclusion (1 minute)

### Key Takeaways
1. **Problem Solved:** Personalized nutrition at scale for Zambian families
2. **Technology:** AI-powered, culturally intelligent platform
3. **Impact:** Health improvement + local economic support
4. **Traction:** 1,200+ beta users, 92% satisfaction rate

### Call to Action
- "We're seeking $200,000 to scale across Lusaka and expand to Ndola"
- "Join us in building a healthier, more prosperous Zambia"
