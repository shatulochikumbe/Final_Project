# Demo Scenarios for ZaNuri 

## Scenario 1: Weight Management Journey
### User Profile: Health-Conscious Mother
- **Name:** Sarah, 35-year-old mother of two
- **Location:** Lusaka, Woodlands
- **Goals:** Lose 10kg, have more energy for her children
- **Budget:** Medium (ZMW 1,500/week for family of 4)
- **Constraints:** Lactose intolerant, limited cooking time

### Demo Flow:
1. **Onboarding:** Show detailed health assessment
2. **Meal Planning:** Generate low-calorie Zambian meals
3. **Shopping:** Create budget-friendly shopping list
4. **Progress:** Track weight loss and energy levels

### Key Features to Highlight:
- Calorie-controlled traditional meals
- Lactose-free recipe alternatives
- Quick preparation recipes (<30 minutes)
- Family-friendly meal options
